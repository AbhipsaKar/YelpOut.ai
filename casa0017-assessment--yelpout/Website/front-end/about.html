We understand the challenges that come with running a restaurant, and we know that it can be difficult to decide on what actionable step to take to improve the quality of your service.

The model we are using is a simple linear model generated by first isolating out the most actionable attributes provided in Yelp's dataset, and using them as predictors for the star ratings associated with each Yelp-listed restaurant.

As Connected Environments MSc students in UCL's Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis, we hope that the model is straightforward and useful. Please get in touch if you have any questions or feedback. We are open to contributions from anyone on GitHub.
